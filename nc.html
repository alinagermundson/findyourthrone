Nosh & Chow

Norrlandsgatan 24

In the lower floor of this classy landmark of a restaurant lies by far one of the most interesting throne rooms here. 19th Century decor everywhere you look, leather-smell refinement blended perfectly with a modern flair and nouveau riche energy; a speakeasy feel in the lounge room outside the throne rooms. the cubicles are covered through and through with a glossy marble with the sink basins embedded with copper deposit. Credit due to the designers opulent interior work.
